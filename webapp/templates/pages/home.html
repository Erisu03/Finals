Latte art

Latte art is a delightful fusion of creativity, skill, and coffee craftsmanship. It's a captivating form of beverage artistry that transforms a simple cup of coffee into a visual masterpiece. Talented baristas pour steamed milk with precision and finesse, meticulously manipulating its flow to create intricate patterns, designs, and shapes atop a smooth canvas of espresso.
